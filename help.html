Help

So in this game, you start off with 15 points. As you progress, you lose 5 points for every move you take. You get more points by collecting coffee, coffee gives you 15 points. If your points reach zero, then you pass out from exhaustion and you cannot progress to work.

\

Moves Instructions

You move around by using the arrow keys, and must reach your workplace (the grey square) before your energy points reach 0.
You cannot move through walls.
